City Challenge Overview:

For many cities, waste collection still relies on fixed schedules and predetermined routes, regardless of whether waste bins are full or nearly empty.
While this approach is simple to implement, it often results in unnecessary fuel consumption, increased operational costs, and inefficient use of collection trucks and personnel.
At the same time, busy areas can experience overflowing bins before the next scheduled collection, leading to public dissatisfaction and sanitation concerns.
The lack of real-time monitoring also means waste collection companies have limited visibility into actual waste levels, making it difficult to respond efficiently to changing demand.

Our Vision

  • Create a waste management system that benefits both residents and businesses as well as waste collection service providers.

  • Foster cooperation between communities and waste collection companies to build a cleaner and more efficient urban environment.

  • Transform waste treatment into a more cost-effective, sustainable, and efficient process through innovative technology.

  • Enable waste to be safely stored near communities without causing discomfort, health concerns, or environmental disturbances.

  • Eliminate the spoilage, odors, and sanitation issues commonly associated with waiting garbage.

  • Provide waste collection companies with greater operational flexibility by reducing dependence on rigid collection schedules.
